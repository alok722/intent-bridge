Build a **Gemini-powered universal bridge** between human intent and complex systems for societal benefit.

The app must accept **any** unstructured, messy, real-world input:
- Voice recordings / speech
- Photos / images (medical scans, accident scenes, food labels)
- Free-form text (messy medical history, incident reports, news snippets)
- Structured feeds (weather APIs, traffic data, sensor readings)
- Documents (PDFs, prescriptions, forms)

And it must instantly convert those inputs into:
- **Structured, validated, actionable outputs** (triage priority, emergency dispatch, medication plan, route rerouting, alert broadcast)
- **Verified facts** (cross-referenced with live data via Grounding)
- **Life-saving or high-impact decisions** surfaced to the right stakeholder in real time

Think: a paramedic uploading a blurry photo of a patient + garbled voice note → Gemini returns a structured triage card + suggested ICD-10 codes + nearest hospital with available ICU beds. Or a city traffic controller pasting a chaotic incident report → Gemini outputs a structured diversion plan with turn-by-turn reroute instructions for emergency vehicles.

The app must demonstrate **at least 3 distinct real-world scenarios** (you pick the most impactful ones, suggestions below).
